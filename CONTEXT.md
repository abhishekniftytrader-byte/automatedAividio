# CONTEXT — Why this project exists

## The goal

Build faceless YouTube channel(s) run by automation, as one of three parallel
income streams (apps / trading / YouTube) before possible job release
~Dec 2026–Jan 2027. Runway: salaried until then — use it to build, not to panic.

## Honest framing (agreed 2026-07-18)

- Faceless YouTube is **saturated**; generic AI-slop channels mostly fail.
- Monetization (YPP) needs 1,000 subs + 4,000 watch-hours (or 10M Shorts views
  in 90 days) — realistically 6–12 months. YouTube is the **slow** stream.
- Secondary payoff matters as much as AdSense: a channel can funnel viewers to
  SignalVisor's paid tier. Content with a funnel beats content alone.
- Differentiator available to us: **real data**. Abhi has genuine backtests
  (LSS, Rizzy, opening-range on gold, etc.) proving popular strategies have no
  edge — honest "I tested it with real data" content is rare in the trading niche.

## Success metrics

| Horizon | Metric |
|---|---|
| Month 1 | Pipeline produces 1 full video end-to-end at $0 cost |
| Month 2–3 | Consistent publishing cadence (target set in ROADMAP) fully automated |
| Month 4–6 | Signal: watch-time retention >40%, subscriber growth trend, first funnel clicks |
| Kill criteria | If after 90 days of consistent uploads there is zero traction signal, we analyze, pivot niche or format — we do not silently keep grinding |
